Privacy Policy

AI Video Clipper is a desktop application used to create short videos from user-selected video files.

The app may use TikTok Login Kit to allow users to connect their TikTok account. When the user connects TikTok, the app may access basic profile information such as open ID, display name, and avatar, only for authentication and account identification.

The app uses the video.upload permission only to upload videos selected or generated by the user to TikTok as drafts. The app does not publish videos automatically without user action.

User video files are processed locally on the user's computer. The app does not sell personal data.

The user remains responsible for reviewing, editing, and posting content inside TikTok.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app owner.
